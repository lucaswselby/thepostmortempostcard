Ade

Ade is an accounting clerk by day, but by night, they spend their spoons in pursuit of creative endeavours. Having tried just about all creative mediums they can think of, Ade proudly admits that they are a writer, specializing in dark fantasy with a special interest in body horror; a painter working with acrylics; and a drawer (if that's even a word). They might be dyslexic, but that has not stopped them from writing and roping their friends into making this zine with them.

Lucas

With his powers of amateur web development and creative writing degree combined, Lucas is Head of Development and the Fiction and Nonfiction editor for The Postmortem Postcard. Of his many accolades, those he deems most relevent are his positions as Reader for Hayden's Ferry Review, Fiction Editor at Superstition Review and Radon Journal, and Script Editor at High Entertainment.

Works: Stories with Legos, A Pleasant Hike, and Guest Post, Fiction Editor Lucas Selby
